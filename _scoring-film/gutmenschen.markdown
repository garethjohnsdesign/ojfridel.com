More than two years later, the actors reunite and pick up where they left off the last time: There is a reunion with the siblings Maryam and Elias. Together with her mobile family structure have set up well in the home of her deceased father. The children are well, new love relationships have emerged. Austria moves to the right and the cousin Yousef receives a negative asylum decision. After two years of growing together, reality breaks down again into the living room, and the fully integrated join the challenge together.
Director Yael Ronen , "the funniest woman in the German-speaking theater" (Spiegel Online) has already won numerous awards. Most recently, she was awarded the International Theater Institute Prize in 2017 as part of the Theater of the World Festival and the Europe Prize for Theatrical Realities, with which the EU Commission honors creative and innovative theatrical projects across genres.
